# Mission Qualifier

## Purpose

Decide whether an AI opportunity is worth pursuing and what proof would make it credible.

## Use When

- the user has a vague AI opportunity
- the first business loop is not clear
- there is risk of AI theater
- the project may be too broad or not-now

## Required Questions

- Who is the operator?
- What trigger starts the work?
- What business object moves through the loop?
- What output proves the work is done?
- What pain or risk makes this worth doing?
- What would make this a no-go?

## Output

Produce a Mission Brief using `skill/templates/mission_brief.md`.

## Stop Condition

Stop when there is a clear go / no-go / not-now posture and a bounded loop candidate.
