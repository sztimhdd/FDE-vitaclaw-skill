# Materials Manifest

This benchmark assumes the customer provides these materials.

## Source Documents

1. Purchase Order (PO)
   - Customer intent
   - Product lines
   - shipment requirements
   - labeling requirements
   - lot number or customer-specific labeling rules

2. Air Waybill (AWB)
   - Logistics truth
   - AWB number
   - flight number
   - routing / destination
   - pieces and gross weight

3. Health Certificate (HC)
   - Compliance truth
   - consignor / consignee
   - official plant approval number
   - BIP / border control point
   - official net and gross weights
   - species and product information
   - transport information

4. Printed Label Image / PDF
   - Final physical output to validate
   - Contains customer-facing shipment and product fields

5. Cross-Check Spreadsheet
   - Customer-maintained rule seed
   - Describes fields, expected sources, cross-check targets, and comments

6. Interview Notes
   - Clarifies what the documents alone do not say
   - Scope boundaries
   - exception rules
   - tolerance expectations
   - customer-specific formats
