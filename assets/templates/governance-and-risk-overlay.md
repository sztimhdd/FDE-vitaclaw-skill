# Governance And Risk Overlay

## Use

Use when the team must make trust, permissions, auditability, and fallback explicit before AI is given operational influence.

## Required Fields

- AI authority level
- Read-only surfaces
- Advisory surfaces
- Gated action surfaces
- Write-capable surfaces
- Human confirmation points
- Audit trail
- Escalation path
- Rollback condition
- Policy or compliance constraints
- Data sensitivity
- Risk owner
- Known unacceptable failure modes

## Optional Fields

- Segmented access rules
- Retention policy
- Model restriction notes

## Typical Anti-Pattern

The system is treated as safe because a human is "somewhere in the loop," but no one defines where authority, evidence, and responsibility actually transfer.

## Completion Standard

The delivery team can explain who is responsible for every AI-influenced action and how that action can be reviewed, stopped, or reversed.
