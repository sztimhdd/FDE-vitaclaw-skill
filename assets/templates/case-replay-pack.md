# Case Replay Pack

## Use

Use when the team needs concrete operational examples before system design becomes abstract.

## Required Fields

- Clean case
- Ugly case
- Disputed case
- Handoff failure
- Shadow workflow observed
- Source artifacts
- Operator involved
- Decision point
- Action taken
- Evidence available at the time
- Outcome
- What this case proves
- What this case does not prove

## Optional Fields

- Frequency estimate
- Reviewer notes
- Missing evidence

## Typical Anti-Pattern

The team relies on stakeholder description, official SOP, or ideal-state process maps without replaying real cases.

## Completion Standard

Another operator should be able to understand how the work actually moves without interviewing the original stakeholder.
