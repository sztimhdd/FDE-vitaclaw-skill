# Expansion Roadmap

## Use

Use after the minimum loop is credible and the team needs a path from pilot to program.

## Required Fields

- Current proven loop
- Replication conditions
- Next adjacent loops
- Capability dependencies
- Organizational dependencies
- Risk ledger
- Stage gates

## Optional Fields

- Platformization path
- Multi-site sequence
- Governance hardening milestones

## Typical Anti-Pattern

The roadmap scales a pilot by ambition instead of by repeatability.

## Completion Standard

The team can explain what must be proven before broader rollout and why the next expansion step is the right one.
