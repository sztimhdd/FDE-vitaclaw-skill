# AI Intervention Design

## Use

Use when selecting the smallest AI surface that can change the business outcome.

## Required Fields

- Target bottleneck
- AI role
- Human role
- Automation boundary
- Fallback path
- Failure modes

## Optional Fields

- Confidence thresholds
- Review policy
- Escalation policy

## Typical Anti-Pattern

AI is added everywhere, but no one can explain why that intervention is the highest-value one.

## Completion Standard

The team can defend why AI is placed here, what authority it has, and how the loop behaves when it is wrong.
