# Scoring Rubric

Total: 100 points.

## 1. Mission Framing — 10

Identifies operator, trigger, business object, output, success proof, and go/no-go posture.

## 2. Reality Capture — 10

Captures clean case, ugly case, handoffs, current artifacts, evidence, and failure points.

## 3. Object Model — 15

Defines business objects, relationships, states, actions, owners, permissions, and evidence.

## 4. Source Authority Reasoning — 15

Maps each critical field or decision to the correct source, conflict policy, tolerance, and review path.

## 5. Comparison / Evaluation Design — 15

Defines field rules, edge cases, thresholds, negative cases, and review evidence.

## 6. Scope Control — 10

Keeps the project to one bounded loop and states non-goals clearly.

## 7. Governance Boundary — 10

Defines human review, permissions, audit, fallback, and rollback expectations.

## 8. PRD Quality — 10

Produces implementation-ready artifacts rather than generic narrative.

## 9. Coding-Agent Handoff — 5

Provides a contract with context, allowed changes, disallowed changes, tasks, checks, evidence, and rollback.

## Pass Thresholds

- 90-100: production-quality skill candidate
- 80-89: MVP pass with human review
- 70-79: needs improvement
- below 70: not operator-grade

## Hard Fail

A run fails if it invents customer facts, hides unknowns, skips source authority reasoning, expands into a broad platform, or claims implementation readiness without checks and evidence.
