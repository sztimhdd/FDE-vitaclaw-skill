# Engagement Modes

## Discovery Sprint

- **When to use**: early qualification, broad opportunity compression, or politically noisy discovery where the team still needs the right loop
- **Starting stage**: Mission Qualification or Operational Reality Capture
- **Required artifacts**:
  - Mission Brief
  - Operational Reality Map
  - Case Replay Pack
  - System Problem Frame
- **Stop condition**: one bounded loop is qualified or explicitly rejected
- **Failure signal**: the team is still discussing broad opportunity space with no named operator or no credible proof path
- **Recommended output depth**: light to medium; usually stop at Mission Qualification or System Framing

## Pilot Design

- **When to use**: the team already has a plausible loop and must define a credible pilot
- **Starting stage**: System Framing or State, Action & Evidence Model
- **Required artifacts**:
  - Minimum Viable Loop
  - POC Acceptance Contract
  - Eval Pack
  - Governance And Risk Overlay
- **Stop condition**: the pilot can be judged, demoed, and governed without renegotiating scope
- **Failure signal**: the pilot still depends on broad architecture promises or has no representative eval cases
- **Recommended output depth**: medium to deep

## Deployment Rescue

- **When to use**: a live or late-stage loop exists but trust, adoption, exceptions, or degradation are breaking the system
- **Starting stage**: Operational Reality Capture
- **Required artifacts**:
  - Operational Reality Map
  - Case Replay Pack
  - Day-2 Operations Plan
  - Governance And Risk Overlay
  - relevant Failure Patterns review
- **Stop condition**: the current break is explained as an operational loop problem with named fixes, owners, and fallback
- **Failure signal**: the team keeps proposing rebuilds without understanding the actual handoff or day-2 failure
- **Recommended output depth**: medium; emphasize reality, rescue path, and operating controls

## Post-Delivery Asset Distillation

- **When to use**: a pilot or delivery loop has produced enough evidence to promote reusable assets
- **Starting stage**: Post-Delivery Overlay
- **Required artifacts**:
  - Asset Distillation Log
  - relevant Eval Pack lessons
  - relevant Day-2 Operations lessons
- **Stop condition**: at least one reusable pattern, eval asset, governance check, or operating template is promoted with an owner
- **Failure signal**: the project closes with narrative debriefs only and no reusable assets
- **Recommended output depth**: medium
